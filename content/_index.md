## About me
---
I am currently a PhD student at Institut Fourier (Grenoble, France) with [Sylvain Gravier](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Sylvain-Gravier) and [Isabelle Sivignon](https://isivigno.github.io).

I am deeply interested in combinatorial optimization, graph theory, and algorithms, with a particular curiosity about problems involving notions of parity.

I am also well-versed in teaching and take great pleasure in sharing mathematics and computer science with diverse audiences, including through outreach and popularization activities.
